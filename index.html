Welcome

Stunned by the urban tumult? Rejuvenate the pure pleasure of being in nature at Pumzika Vacation House.

Established in 1942 and passed from fathers to sons for three generations now, Pumzika Vacation House, also known as Bondu Cottages, is a family business that has built a clientele faithful and always growing, this, thanks to an advertisement whose letters of nobility are no longer to do again: "word of mouth"!

Based near the city of Nakuru in the beautiful Elementaita, known for its abundant greenery and vast expanses of water, the twelve residences of Pumzika Vacation House line the shores of Lake Naivasha, which communicates less than a kilometer away from the Lake Nakuru National Park, one of the major tourist attractions in Kenya.

Appreciated for his personalized service, his warm contact and the very cleanliness of the place - his distinctive mark - this holiday resort where calm and simplicity welcome you will know unmistakably charm you!

The panoramic view of the lake and mountains offered by each cottage will enchant you, as well as as the intimacy of providing adequately isolated homes.

Member of the Magical Kenya , Pumzika Vacation House will seduce you whether for a holiday with family, friends, for a stay of unforgettable fishing or for the tranquility that characterizes the places and the relaxation they arouse.

In a few words, you will have a "blow of heart" assured for this corner of rest!
