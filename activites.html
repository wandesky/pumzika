Trout fishing

To liven up your stay and vary your activities, Pumzika Vacation House holds a lake with right exclusive specially designed for trout fishing. Less than 15 minutes from the cottage, we will drive there and you provide a boat with electric motor, for a day of calm in private land.

  • Types of trout: speckled and rainbow
  • Transport included
  • Equipment: boat, electric motor, battery
  • Fishing hours: full day or according to your needs

Please contact us for our special rates for cottage residents.

The hunt

Private land, without exclusive rights. It is possible to have access to sites baited with caches.

For the regulations and hunting periods in force according to game types and zones, please refer to the Delamare Ranch hunting guidelines. Please note that we are part of Delamare Ranch.
