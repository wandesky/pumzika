ACCOMMODATION AND SERVICES

Activity period: from mid-April to 20 November

Member of the Magical Kenya , The company has 12 cottages, some of which accommodate up to 14 people (2 to 7 double beds). The cottages are all provided with electricity and in hot water.

In addition to cottage rentals, we also rent boats and outboard motors for walking and fishing.

  • Boats: 14 aluminum feet
  • Motors: 9.9Hp and 15Hp

ANIMALS

Being one of the few establishments to accept animals, at no additional cost, we are aware that holidays without our hairy friends are not so enjoyable, and especially, ask for more pre-departure logistics.

We insist, however, on leaving the premises in acceptable conditions when you leave the cottage: a good sweep to eliminate the hair, go around the field for do not leave any excrement and do not let the animals climb on the furniture (couches and beds).

To make your stay enjoyable, we thank you for your understanding.
